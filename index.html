Welcome to EPS Young Minds Groningen

EPS Young Minds Groningen is the local section of the European Physical Society - Young Minds project. We bring students together to explore physics and related disciplines through seminars, workshops, lab visits, and community outreach. Our goal is to connect students with academia and industry, spark curiosity, and create a welcoming network for everyone who loves science.
